# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 09.09.2024

This Privacy Policy explains how Garanty Studios collects, uses, and protects your data when you use our services, including Garanty Connect.

---

## 1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information such as:

- Name  
- Email address  
- Location  
- User IDs  

This data is used only for:

- Account management  
- Providing core services  

---

## 2. Collection of Financial Information

When you make in-app purchases, we may collect payment-related information.

This is used solely to:

- Process transactions securely  

All payment data is handled according to standard industry security practices.

---

## 3. Device Information

We may collect device identifiers such as:

- Advertising ID  
- Android ID  

This helps us:

- Improve our services  
- Provide personalized ads via Unity Ads  

---

## 4. Location Data

We may collect approximate location data in order to:

- Improve user experience  
- Provide localized content and features  

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We only share data with the following services:

- Unity Ads (advertising personalization)  
- PlayFab (account management)  

No other third parties receive your data.

---

## 6. Data Security

Your data is stored securely and protected using industry-standard measures, including encryption.

We follow applicable data protection regulations to safeguard:

- Personal data  
- Financial information  

---
